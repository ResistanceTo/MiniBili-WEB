# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** October 12, 2025

## 1. Introduction

MiniBili is a free, ad-free third-party Bilibili client for Apple platforms. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

## 2. Data Collection

**We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any user data.**

Specifically:

- ❌ **No Personal Information**: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any identifying credentials.
- ❌ **No Usage Analytics**: We do not track your watch history, browsing behavior, or app usage patterns.
- ❌ **No Third-Party SDKs**: The app contains zero analytics, tracking, or telemetry SDKs.
- ❌ **No Intermediate Servers**: We do not operate any backend servers. All network requests go directly to Bilibili's official API endpoints.

## 3. Local Storage

The app stores minimal necessary configuration locally on your Apple device. This data never leaves your device:

- **Login credentials** (stored securely in iOS Keychain to maintain your login session)
- **App preferences** and settings
- **Temporary cached images** and video data

You can clear all local data at any time simply by uninstalling the application.

## 4. Third-Party Services

MiniBili uses Bilibili's official API to retrieve video and community feeds. When using the app:

- Your device communicates directly with Bilibili's official servers.
- Requests are subject to Bilibili's Privacy Policy.
